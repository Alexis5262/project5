Top SuperBowl Commericals

NFL 100 Super Bowl Commerical

Source: YouTube - Ad Meter 2019: NFL By:USA TODAY

The NFL 100 Super Bowl commercial, which aired during Super Bowl LIII in 2019, celebrated the league's 100-year history by bringing together a star-studded cast of NFL legends, past and present. The ad featured iconic players like Tom Brady, Peyton Manning, Jerry Rice, and many others, along with comedic moments and nods to memorable football moments. The commercial, set in a high-energy, action-packed scenario, emphasized the rich legacy and evolution of the game while showcasing the players' enduring impact on football culture. It was a powerful tribute to the sport’s history, blending humor and nostalgia to honor the NFL's century-long journey

Tubi Interface Interruption

Source: YouTube - Tubi Interface Interruption|Super Bowl By:Phil Prasek

The commercial starts like any typical Super Bowl ad—glamorous, cinematic, and well-produced. Just when you think you're watching another high-budget, dramatic ad, the screen suddenly glitches and switches to the Tubi interface. This disruption catches viewers off guard and immediately breaks the fourth wall. The initial confusion—“Did I accidentally change the channel?”—creates a sense of bewilderment that's both surprising and relatable. This unexpected shift is a classic setup for comedy, playing on the universal experience of technological mishaps.

Super Bowl State Farm Commerical

Source: YouTube - SUPER BOWL STATE FARM COMMERCIAL (FEATURING: DRAKE, PATRICK MAHOMES, AND AARON RODGERS By:King M

The State Farm Super Bowl commercial featuring Drake, Patrick Mahomes, and Aaron Rodgers (aired during Super Bowl LV in 2021) is a humorous and star-studded ad that plays on the theme of "State Farm makes things simple." In the commercial, Drake appears as a "good neighbor" offering his assistance to Mahomes and Rodgers in various humorous scenarios. The ad is packed with playful exchanges, including Mahomes and Rodgers teasing each other while Drake seamlessly steps in to resolve their issues with State Farm's services. With its blend of celebrity appeal, quick wit, and comedic moments, the commercial highlights State Farm's customer service while showcasing the charisma of the three stars.
